Matthew S. Bliwise serves as Vice President and Underwriting Counsel for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. As an experienced attorney, he manages complex single- and multi-site transactions involving all types of commercial properties throughout the country. Matthew is also a frequent speaker at the International Council of Shopping Centers annual Law Conference.

❝

At WestendTitles we specialize in shepherding every transaction from initial order placement until we cross the finish line at closing. Because of the complexities of insuring commercial matters, our team stands next to our clients every step of the way in order to facilitate a smooth deal process.


Prior to his employment with WestendTitles, he practiced law at Morgan, Lewis & Bockius LLP in New York City. Matthew received his undergraduate degree, magna cum laude, from Williams College and his law degree, cum laude, from New York University School of Law, where he was a member of the Moot Court Board.
