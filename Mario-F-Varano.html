Mario F. Varano serves as Vice President, Sales for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. He has over 20 years of experience in the sale and marketing of title insurance and is responsible for commercial originations throughout the country. Working on a national level, Mario and his staff have vast experience in the pricing of complex multi-site transactions and coordinating all phases in facilitating product output.

❝

As part of the largest title insurance underwriter in the country gives us the ability to tap the expertise of the most talented and knowledgeable people in the industry.


Mario’s Clients tend to include law firms, commercial developers and advisors, commercial lenders, institutional and private equity investors, along with a strong focus within the power and utility markets.
