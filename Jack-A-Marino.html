Jack Marino serves as Executive Vice President, Counsel, Executive Sales for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. He previously served as Assistant General Counsel of Teachers Insurance and Annuity Association.  Jack is a member of the American College of Real Estate Lawyers and the American College of Mortgage Attorneys. Jack is also a member and former Governor of the Anglo-American Real Property Institute.

❝

I take pride in our strong, longstanding relationships. Our commitment to maintaining and strengthening those relationships is the core of our philosophy. This, together with our in-depth understanding of the business and willingness to partner with our customers towards resolutions of issues, assures our customers receive an unparalleled level of service and protection.


Jack is a former Adjunct Assistant Professor of Real Estate for the New York University School of Continuing Education. He has frequently lectured and authored articles on title insurance, real estate and finance.
