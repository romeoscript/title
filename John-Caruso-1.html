John Caruso serves as Vice President and Counsel for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. He entered the industry in 1999 working at Title Associates which was purchased by Stewart Title Insurance Company in 2005. At Title Associates, John acted as counsel and in a sales capacity. He joined the New York NCS sales department in May of 2011. 

❝

Our network of superior professionals provides a wealth of expertise unparalleled in the title industry.


Prior to entering the title industry John spent nine years at large New York City law firms including five years at Skadden Arps working on large real estate transactions such as purchases and sales, loans and leases. Prior to entering the legal field he was a New York City police officer for six and a half years.
