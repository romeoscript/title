Patricia Lopinski serves as Vice President of Sales for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. She is a highly respected professional who offers over 20 years of experience in the field and a year-over-year record of exemplary client satisfaction. With a broad range of knowledge in real estate products and services, Pat’s background covers the gamut of transactions, from single-site deals in New York City to larger, multi-site transactions nationwide. She has built a solid client base and generates 80-90% of her current business from repeat clients, with most of the remaining business coming from client referrals. Pat’s clients know she will do whatever it takes to meet their critical requirements.

❝

WestendTitles is the leader in financial strength providing a platform for serving our clients and offering solutions for transactions locally and nationally, including our niche: large complex transactions.


Pat is proud to be a team member within WestendTitles, a company with a strong history that offers expert-level services for complex underwriting, escrow, and other need areas. Within the company, she frequently serves as a key liaison and go-to person for a variety of initiatives, providing the strategies to resolve client issues. Pat is a trusted, valued team member who can work effectively with all involved in the real estate lifecycle, including developers, investors, and attorneys. She is fully capable and prepared to help prospective clients with all of their real estate transaction needs.
