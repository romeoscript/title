Lisa Zicchinella serves as National Underwriter for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. She began with the company in March of 2004 as a national coordinator/assistant for large multi-site/state transactions. In 2010, Lisa was promoted to National Underwriter with responsibilities that include coordination/closing of single site commercial transactions, and multi-site/state transactions over $5 million.  Additionally, she assists in the coordination of title comments for buyer/borrower, seller and lender’s counsels, coordination and recordation of documents, coordination of pro formas, review of escrow provisions/PSA’s, instruction letters, high liability approval entry, and review of recordable documents.  Lisa closely coordinates with our local offices as a national business unit to close the transaction smoothly.  Additionally, she works on escrow/settlement and disbursements for my transactions.

❝

I believe in always putting the customer first by providing the highest level of customer service, remaining attentive to the customer’s changing needs in the transaction and being a proactive facilitator in order to provide them with the smoothest closing possible.


Lisa received the Company’s Circle of Excellence Award in June of 2007 and participated in and completed The Leadership Program from 2006/2007. She earned a bachelor’s degree from The College of New Jersey in 1998. Lisa enjoys traveling, reading, cross stitching, and spending time with family and friends.
