Michelle Cruz Bingham serves as Vice President, Sales for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. With nearly 10 years of experience as underwriting counsel, she brings a wealth of knowledge in closing complex, high profile real estate transactions in New York. Prior to her time in the title industry, Michelle was a practicing real estate attorney at several New York law firms representing lenders, borrowers and developers, and was in-house counsel at a large pension fund company. Her legal experience gives Michelle an understanding of the challenges faced in closing a real estate transaction and enables her to provide solutions in a timely manner to meet clients' deadlines.

❝

I am dedicated to providing a smooth closing process for our clients with attention to detail, superior service and follow up. I have the great fortune of working with the best team of underwriters and title professionals to achieve this goal.


Michelle did her undergraduate work at University of Colorado in Boulder and received her law degree from Fordham University School of Law. She is a member of the Fordham Law Alumni Association and is a volunteer at the Feerick Center for Social Justice.
