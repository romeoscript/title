Vice President and Underwriting Counsel   |  
Commercial Underwriting
New York, NY

CONTACT JESSIE

Jessie serves as Vice President and Underwriting Counsel for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. She is responsible for managing all aspects of commercial closings on single and multi-site/multi-state real estate transactions. Jessie’s professionalism, dedication, and ability to build effective working relationships and provide practical solutions make her an asset to WestendTitles.  

❝

I am proud to be part of a team whose collaborative efforts deliver the highest quality of service to our clients. Every effort is made to proactively resolve title issues and anticipate our clients’ needs to ensure a smooth closing process.


Prior to joining WestendTitles, she spent 15 years as Vice President and Counsel at Title Associates, a commercial title operation, and headed its national practice. In addition to handling multi-site/multi-state commercial transactions, Jessie closed commercial transactions involving wind farms and power generation facilities.

Jessie received a B.A. from Fordham University and received her J.D. from New York Law School. She is a member of the New York State Bar Association.
