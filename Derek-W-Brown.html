Derek W. Brown serves as Vice President and Production Manager for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. Derek joined the company in 1986. His current focus is on managing all local title processes after the initial title report is completed until each file closes and the documents are recorded.

❝

Regardless of the transaction, small or large, the satisfaction of all customers is paramount to achieving success. I strive to provide attention and accountability throughout all facets of the title process to ensure the successful completion of each transaction.


Derek  participated in the Company’s Leadership Development Program. He has an MBA in business management from St. Peter’s University and is a member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc.
