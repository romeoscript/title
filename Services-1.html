Services


Check Out Our Available Services

Commercial real estate investments require extensive knowledge of the financial, legal and regulatory aspects of buying and selling income property. WestendTitles’s National Commercial Services unit located in New York, New York is uniquely positioned to partner with large enterprises and expedite complex transactions, including multi-site, multi-state, and even international dealings.

As your partner in all commercial real estate transactions within New York City and in all 50 states, our team of professionals offers best-in-class services in title, escrow, closing and post-closing, electronic document delivery and recording, tax-deferred property exchanges, UCCPlus, and more. We deliver peace of mind while building lifelong relationships.

1031 Exchange

Proven Exchange Solutions
A tax deferred exchange is a simple strategy that involves “exchanging” one “like kind” property for another for a deferred tax gain treatment. We provide best-in-class Qualified Intermediary services through IPX for the proper acquisition and relinquishment of properties according to section 1031 of the IRC.

UCCPlus Insurance

Personal Property Title Insurance
UCCPlus is title insurance tailored to protect a commercial lender’s security interest in non-real estate transactions, including: mezzanine financing, equity pledges, project finance/energy loans, revolving/term/working capital loans, all asset loans, construction loans, and mixed collateral transactions. In select states, coverage for the purchase of an interest in a limited liability company, limited partnership or corporation is available.
