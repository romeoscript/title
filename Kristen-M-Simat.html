Kristen Simat serves as Underwriting Counsel for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. She joined WestendTitles in 2013 and currently handles both commercial and residential transactions.

❝

Working closely with clients to reach their goal is paramount to our success.


Prior to joining WestendTitles, Kristen was National Clearance Counsel for Title Associates. She also worked for a boutique Italian law firm representing Italian clients in the purchase and sale of New York properties.

Kristen participated as a CLE panelist on bankruptcy matters entitled, The Effects of Bankruptcy on Real Property Transactions. Kristen received a B.A. in Sociology from Fordham University and a J.D. from New York Law School. She is a member of the New York Bar Association and is fluent in Italian
