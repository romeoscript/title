Elliot L. Hurwitz serves as Vice President, Sales for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. He previously served as Chief Commercial Counsel for the Company for two decades. A former partner at Weil, Gotshal & Manges, LLP, Elliot joined WestendTitles in 2004. He is a member of the New York State and New York City bar associations and is a Fellow, American College of Real Estate Lawyers.

❝

I have an unwavering commitment to our clients and they appreciate the clarity, timeliness, and effectiveness of my counsel. I have become, for many, their first call for solutions to their toughest challenges.


Elliot is Chairman, The Commercial Real Estate Institute (Practicing Law Institute’s 2 day seminar for real estate attorneys). He is also a contributor to the following publications: New York Law Journal, Commercial Leasing Law & Strategy, Shopping Center Legal Update, Metropolitan Corporate Counsel, Real Estate Weekly, The Corporate Counsellor’s Deskbook, and New Appleman New York Insurance Law. In addition, Elliot is a frequent lecturer at law firms, financial institutions and trade associations on real estate finance and title insurance.
