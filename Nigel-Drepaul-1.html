Nigel Drepaul serves as Senior Vice President, Sales for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. He has been in the title industry for over 29 years starting with Ticor Title in 1985 as an accounting clerk and then moving to WestendTitles sales department in 1987.  In his current position, Nigel works very closely with his production and underwriting team to provide the best service in the industry.

❝

I take much pride in providing excellent customer service to my clients.


Nigel lives in Bayside with his wife Camille and their five children.
