New York NCS

Commercial Underwriting

CONTACT MARK

• 212-880-1240

Mark serves as National Underwriter for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. Joining the industry in 2013, he has experience working on large multi-state, multi-site portfolios for a variety of clients including national and local developers, institutional lenders, large law firms, and direct investors. Mark has worked on hundreds of transactions involving, but not limited to, the sale and financing of multi-family buildings, industrial facilities, self-storage facilities, office buildings, solar farms, hotels, new development, and retail properties. He is proud to be part of a team that exhibits excellent qualities across the board providing our clients with the best possible experience for all transactions.

❝

To be truly successful, I apply my top to bottom philosophy of providing each client with excellent service and expertise tailored to their needs.


Mark has a B.A. degree from Binghamton University. He resides in New York City and enjoys traveling, reading, and spending time with friends.
