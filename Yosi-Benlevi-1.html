New York NCS

Commercial Underwriting
New York, NY

CONTACT JOE

Yosi (Joe) Benlevi serves as Vice President and Senior Underwriting Counsel for WestendTitles’s New York National Commercial Services (NCS) operation. He underwrites complex multi-site, multi-state commercial transactions, including shopping centers, hotels, office buildings and power plants, and handles the complicated structuring and underwriting issues of such transactions. Prior to joining WestendTitles in 2005, Joe headed the legal department of one of the largest title agents in New York State.

❝

I am proud to be part of a team that handles complicated national transactions and provides creative solutions in the most professional and customer oriented way possible.


Joe is admitted to practice law in New York and Israel and holds an LLB (1995) and LLM (2000) degrees from Tel-Aviv University. He is a member of the New York State Land Title Association Legislative Committee, as well as board member of the American Friends of Tel Aviv University, and has served as a member of the New York Title Insurance Rate Service Association, Inc. (TIRSA), Law & Forms Committee.
